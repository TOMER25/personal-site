// ABOUT ME

// NOW: Researching my thesis on captivity, memory, and meaning among the German POWs at Camp Rupert, Idaho.

Hello and welcome to my website. I'm Will Tomer, a graduate student at Idaho State University, currently pursuing my Master's degree in History. Before returning to academia, I spent several years working in print journalism — I wrote and edited opinion pieces on technology and data privacy, global affairs, and more for papers in the Rust Belt.

My academic research interests include social and sensory histories of the Third Reich, as well as German post-war reconstruction. I also study media, politics, technology, film, sports, and the craft of writing.

This page is my little corner of the internet. Feel free to poke around.
